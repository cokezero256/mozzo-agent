# Knowledge: ContentOps Methodology & SOPs

> Mavi loads this for script review, content feedback, and quality control skills.

---

## THE CONTENTOPS FORMULA

Every video Mozzo delivers must hit this formula:

```
Music (50%) + Visual Editing (30%) + Emotional Connection (20%) = ContentOps
```

**Music is the biggest lever.** Most agencies ignore it. We don't.
- Music sets the emotional tone before any word is spoken
- Must match the energy arc of the video (build → peak → resolution)
- Never use generic YouTube library tracks on premium clients

**Visual editing is the frame.** It supports, never distracts.
- Cuts must serve the story, not show off the editor
- Text overlays: clean, minimal, on-brand
- B-roll: purposeful, not filler

**Emotional connection is the output.** If the viewer doesn't feel something, the video failed.

---

## SCRIPT STRUCTURE: WHY → WHAT → HOW

Every long-form video script follows this structure:

1. **WHY** — Why does this matter to the viewer? (Hook + stakes)
2. **WHAT** — What are you actually going to teach/show? (Promise)
3. **HOW** — The actual content delivery (Meat of the video)

---

## HOOK RULES

- **Max 100 words** for the hook section
- **Pattern interrupt in first 3 seconds** — visual or audio surprise
- Hook must answer: "Why should I keep watching THIS video RIGHT NOW?"
- Never start with "In today's video I'm going to..."
- Never start with the creator's name or channel intro

---

## CTA: THE NETFLIX METHOD

- Never ask viewers to subscribe — it signals desperation
- Instead: tease the NEXT video at the end ("If you want to see how I did X, that video is right here")
- Make the next video feel like the obvious continuation
- Goal: autoplay behavior, not subscribe behavior

---

## SHORT-FORM: VIRALER METHODOLOGY

For Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts:

1. **Ideation** — Find the specific, personal, counterintuitive angle
2. **Validation** — Does this have viral potential? Check: search volume, social proof, pain/desire trigger
3. **Hook selection** — 3 hook options per idea, pick the most scroll-stopping
4. **Script** — Max 60 seconds. Hook (0-3s) → Payoff (3-50s) → CTA (50-60s)
5. **Format** — Talking head, screen record, or B-roll overlay? Match to content type
