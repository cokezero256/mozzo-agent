## Welcome to Mozzo

You edit for YouTube creators who are building businesses, not just channels.
Every video you deliver represents their brand, their credibility, and their revenue.
That means quality is non-negotiable — and speed matters too.

The ContentOps formula: **Music (50%) + Visual Editing (30%) + Emotional Connection (20%)**

If you internalize one thing, make it this: the music you choose and how you pace
the emotion in a video matters more than any technical edit decision.

## Workflow: Every Single Task

### 1. Receive task in ClickUp
- Task assigned to you → you get a notification
- Read the full brief in the task description
- Check: deadline, client name, video type, any special instructions

### 2. Download raw footage
- Go to the client's Drive folder → `02_Raw Footage/`
- Download the file labeled for your task
- If footage is missing: comment in ClickUp immediately, do NOT wait

### 3. Edit
- Follow `video-editing-standards.md` for all technical standards
- Follow the client's style guide (in their Drive folder `01_Brand Assets/`)
- If no style guide exists (new client): follow the brief exactly, flag any uncertainty

### 4. Self-review before upload
Run through the `quality-checklist.md` — every item, every time.
Do not skip this. It exists because people skip it and then videos get rejected.

### 5. Upload and deliver
- Upload to client Drive: `03_Delivered Videos/`
- File name: `[ClientName]_[VideoTitle]_[YYYY-MM-DD]_v1.mp4`
- Export thumbnail frame → upload to `04_Thumbnails/`
- In ClickUp: move task to **"Pending Review"**
- Add a comment: `Delivered. [Drive link]. Thumbnail: [Drive link]. Timecode for thumbnail: [XX:XX]`

### 6. Revisions
- If revision requested: read the feedback carefully before re-editing
- Upload as `v2.mp4` (or v3 for second revision)
- Move task back to "Pending Review" and comment again

## Communication Rules

**Response time:**
- Slack messages from Carolina or Mauricio: respond within 2 hours during business hours
- ClickUp task assignments: acknowledge within 4 hours

**If you have a blocker (missing footage, unclear brief, technical issue):**
- Comment in the ClickUp task immediately — do NOT wait
- DM Carolina directly if the deadline is within 24 hours
- Never miss a deadline without flagging it in advance

**Business hours:** 9am–6pm your local time, Mon–Fri
**Urgent situations:** DM Carolina, she will escalate if needed

## Quality = Your Reputation

At Mozzo, editors are evaluated on:
1. **On-time delivery rate** — did you hit the deadline?
2. **Revision rate** — how often does your work come back?
3. **Client satisfaction** — does the client love your edits?

Two rejections on the same video triggers a review with Carolina.
Consistent late delivery triggers a tier review.

Excellent work gets you more assignments, better clients, and tier upgrades.
