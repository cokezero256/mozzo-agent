# SOP: Pre-Delivery Quality Checklist

> Run this BEFORE moving any task to "Pending Review" in ClickUp.
> No exceptions. No shortcuts.

---

## AUDIO

- [ ] Voice is clear — no background noise, no room echo, no distortion
- [ ] Audio levels: YouTube = -14 LUFS, Social = -16 LUFS
- [ ] Peak: never exceeds -1 dBTP
- [ ] Music is mixed UNDER voice (music at -18 to -22 LUFS)
- [ ] No audio pops, clicks, or sudden volume jumps
- [ ] Transitions between music tracks are smooth (no abrupt cuts)

## MUSIC

- [ ] Music matches the emotional tone of the content
- [ ] Music builds with the video (not flat throughout)
- [ ] Source is licensed (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, Musicbed, or client-provided)
- [ ] Music does not distract from the speaker

## VIDEO / PACING

- [ ] No dead air (pauses > 1.5 seconds cut)
- [ ] No filler words left in (um, uh, like — unless intentional)
- [ ] B-roll is purposeful (not random stock footage)
- [ ] No shot longer than 8 seconds without a cut or overlay (long-form)
- [ ] Pacing feels natural — not rushed, not dragging

## COLOR

- [ ] Color grade applied and consistent with client's previous videos
- [ ] No exposure/color issues in raw footage left uncorrected
- [ ] LUT applied consistently across all clips

## TEXT & CAPTIONS

- [ ] All text overlays are spelled correctly
- [ ] Text is readable on mobile screen (not too small)
- [ ] Text duration: minimum 1.5 seconds on screen
- [ ] No more than 7 words per text overlay
- [ ] Captions reviewed for accuracy (if applicable)

## EXPORT & DELIVERY

- [ ] Exported at correct resolution (1080p minimum)
- [ ] File named correctly: `[ClientName]_[VideoTitle]_[YYYY-MM-DD]_v1.mp4`
- [ ] Uploaded to correct client Drive folder: `03_Delivered Videos/`
- [ ] Thumbnail frame exported at full resolution (1920x1080 min)
- [ ] Thumbnail uploaded to: `04_Thumbnails/`
- [ ] Thumbnail timecode noted in ClickUp comment

## CLICKUP

- [ ] Task moved to "Pending Review"
- [ ] Delivery comment added with Drive link + thumbnail link + thumbnail timecode

---

**If any item is unchecked: fix it before delivering.**
**If you're unsure about any item: ask Carolina before delivering.**
