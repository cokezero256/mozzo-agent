# SOP: Client Onboarding

> Version 1.0 — March 2026
> Owner: Mauricio
> Timeline: Complete within 72 hours of signing

---

## Overview

Onboarding is where retention is won or lost. The client just paid. They need to feel:
1. The decision was right
2. We are already working
3. They are in expert hands

Every step below exists to reinforce those three feelings.

---

## Phase 1: Immediate (Within 2 hours of signing)

### Step 1: Welcome Message
Send a personalized Slack or email message. Do NOT use a generic template.
Reference: their niche, their goal, something specific from the sales call.

Template (customize every line):
```
Hey [Name],

Welcome to Mozzo — really excited to work on [specific goal they mentioned].

Here's what happens next:
1. I'll send you the onboarding form within the hour
2. Onboarding call is scheduled for [date/time]
3. Your first video will be in editing within [X] days of the call

Before the call, the most helpful thing you can do is send me:
- 3 videos you love (yours or from other creators in your niche)
- Your brand guide or preferred fonts/colors (if you have one)
- Access to your Google Drive / Dropbox folder

Talk soon,
Mauricio
```

### Step 2: Create ClickUp Project
- Duplicate the "New Client Template" project in ClickUp
- Rename: `[ClientName] — [Month Year]`
- Assign Carolina as project manager
- Set first task due date: 3 days from today

### Step 3: Create Client Drive Folder
Structure:
```
[ClientName]/
├── 01_Brand Assets/
│   ├── Logos/
│   ├── Fonts/
│   └── Color Palette/
├── 02_Raw Footage/
├── 03_Delivered Videos/
├── 04_Thumbnails/
└── 05_Scripts/
```
Share folder with client and notify them of the link.

## Phase 2: Onboarding Call (Within 48 hours)

### Pre-Call Checklist
- [ ] Client has received the Drive folder link
- [ ] Client has access to the onboarding form
- [ ] Review their channel/content before the call (minimum 5 recent videos)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 specific observations about their current content

### Onboarding Call Agenda (45-60 min)

**1. Welcome & context (5 min)**
- Confirm their goal and timeline
- Brief overview of how Mozzo works (ContentOps formula)

**2. Content audit (15 min)**
- Review their best-performing and worst-performing recent videos
- Identify: what's working, what's missing, what we're going to change
- This is where you demonstrate expertise — be specific

**3. Style guide creation (15 min)**
- Establish: color grade preference, music direction, pacing preference
- Review reference videos they sent
- Set the standard for the first video

**4. Workflow walkthrough (10 min)**
- ClickUp: how they'll see task status
- Drive: where raw footage goes, where delivered videos land
- Communication: Slack preferred, response time expectations
- Revision policy: 2 rounds included, what counts as a revision

**5. First video (10 min)**
- Choose the first video topic together (or confirm what they've already shot)
- Set the delivery date expectation
- Confirm next raw footage upload timeline

### Post-Call Actions (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Send call summary + key decisions made
- [ ] Create style guide doc in client Drive folder
- [ ] Add first video brief to ClickUp
- [ ] Assign editor (JD-A for first video — always)

---

## Phase 3: First Video (Within 72 hours of raw footage receipt)

The first video is the most important video Mozzo will ever make for this client.
It sets the standard. It either confirms their confidence or creates doubt.

**Rules for the first video:**
- JD-A editor only
- Mauricio reviews before delivery (every time, no exceptions)
- Deliver 24 hours BEFORE the committed deadline
- Include a short Loom walkthrough explaining key edit decisions

**First video delivery message:**
```
Hey [Name],

Your first video is ready: [Drive link]

Quick notes on what we did:
- [Music choice and why]
- [Pacing/cut decisions]
- [Any specific ContentOps choices made]

You have 2 rounds of revisions included. Let me know what you think.

[Name], the goal is to lock in the style guide from this video so every
future video flows faster. The more specific your feedback, the better.
```

---

## Revision Policy

| Round | What's included | Timeline |
|-------|----------------|---------|
| Round 1 | Any changes to pacing, music, text, color | Within 24 hours of feedback |
| Round 2 | Refinements based on Round 1 | Within 24 hours of feedback |
| Round 3+ | Billable at $[rate]/hour | Quoted before starting |

**What counts as a revision:**
- Any change to the edit (cut, music swap, text edit, color change)

**What does NOT count as a revision:**
- Export in a different format/resolution (free)
- Minor caption corrections (free, up to 5)

## 30-Day Check-In

At 30 days, Mavi automatically flags for a CS check-in (see `skills/customer-success.md`).
Mauricio reviews:
- Delivery rate: did we hit every deadline?
- Satisfaction signals: any complaints? Any compliments?
- Growth opportunity: are they ready for more volume or additional services?
